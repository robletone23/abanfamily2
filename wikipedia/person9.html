Dioscoro Rocero Aban

Born: November 4, 1990

Occupation: Barista

Dioscoro Rocero Aban, the 7th son of Diosdado and Rosario Aban, was born in Naga, Cebu. He later moved to Manila, where he pursued and completed marine studies. After working as a marine for a year, he had to stop due to a shortage of agencies. He then found an opportunity as a barista in Saudi Arabia, where he worked for five years. Dioscoro is currently not married but shares a life with Jhana Reyes Marcel, with whom he has one daughter. He continues to work hard to provide for his family.
