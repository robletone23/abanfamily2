Domingo Rocero Aban

Born: May 20, 1989

Occupation: Ride-Hailing Driver

Domingo Rocero Aban, the sixth son of Diosdado and Rosario Aban, was born in Naga, Cebu. He later moved to Manila, where he pursued a career in marine studies. After successfully finishing his marine course, Domingo worked as a marine for two years. Despite gaining valuable experience, life struggles led him to step away from the marine industry. In 2022, Domingo married Marejean Dignos Aban, and they are now proud parents of one son and one daughter. He continues to work hard to provide for his family, dedicating his efforts to ensuring their well-being
