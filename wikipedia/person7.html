Christina Rocero Aban - Palomares

Born: October 27, 1987

Occupation: Housewife

Christina Rocero Aban, the fifth daughter of Diosdado and Rosario Aban, was born in Naga, Cebu. She later moved to Manila, where she focused on securing a stable job to build a strong foundation for her future. In 2016, Christina married Dennis Palomares, and together they have one daughter. Known for her dedication to her family and work, Christina has built a stable and loving household while balancing her responsibilities with grace.
