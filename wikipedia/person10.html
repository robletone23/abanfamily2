Letecia Rocero Aban

Born: May 20, 1995

Occupation: Cashier

Letecia Rocero Aban, the 8th daughter of Diosdado and Rosario Aban, was born in Naga, Cebu, and later moved to Manila. She pursued a degree in office administration and successfully completed her studies. Letecia became a cashier and has been dedicated to working for the same company for five years. A religious person with a great sense of humor, Letecia is known for being a funny and loving sister. Currently single, she works hard to support her brother Diosdado's tuition, demonstrating her commitment to family and her perseverance in providing for them.
