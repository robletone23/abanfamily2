Bernadith Rocero Aban - Lago

Born: June 11, 1985

Occupation: Business Women

Bernadith Rocero Aban-Lago, the third daughter of Diosdado and Rosario Aban, was born in Naga, Cebu. Growing up, she was known for being a very shy person, but over time, she evolved into a confident and determined woman. After moving to Manila, she pursued a career in business, driven by her desire to provide for her family. In 2010, Bernadith married Jereon Lago, and together they had a son. Her journey into business has been motivated by her commitment to ensuring a bright future for her child. Despite facing personal challenges, including her divorce in 2023, Bernadith remains strong, continuing to focus on her business and providing for her son. Known for her loving and caring nature, Bernadith's resilience and dedication to her family define her. She continues to work hard for their well-being, embodying both strength and compassion in her daily life..
