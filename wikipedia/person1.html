Diosdado Gabato Aban Sr.

Born: August 10, 1958 - October 30, 2015

Occupation: N/A

Diosdado G. Aban Sr., or known as "Dadong", was born in Naga, Cebu. From a young age, he displayed a remarkable talent for craftsmanship, creating intricate rings. As he grew older, his versatile skill set expanded, and he became known for his exceptional driving abilities and proficiency in home repairs. After marrying Rosario, Dadong took on the role of a jeepney driver, navigating the bustling streets with a professional flair. His driving skills were not just about getting from point A to point B; they were a testament to his dedication and commitment to providing for his family. Dadong is not only a skilled driver and handyman but also a man who deeply values relationships. He is known for his love of beer, which he enjoys sharing with family and friends during gatherings. His humor and lovable personality make him the life of the party, bringing joy and laughter to those around him. Throughout his life, Dadong has embraced his flexible skills, tackling various challenges at home and beyond. Whether fixing a leaky faucet or entertaining friends with his jokes, he embodies the spirit of resilience and warmth, making him a beloved figure in his community and family..
