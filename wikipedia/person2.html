Rosario Rocero Aban

Born: October 27, 1960

Occupation: Retired

Rosario R. Aban was born in Mindanao, Surigao. She later moved to Cebu, where she met Diosdado. The two fell in love and began their life together. Eventually, they moved to Manila, where Rosario became a housekeeper, showcasing her hard work and dedication to her family. A loving and caring mother, she nurtures her family with compassion and support. Rosario has a passion for gardening and loves to plant, cultivating beauty and life around her home. Her strong faith guides her values and actions, making her a deeply religious person. Rosario is known for her sense of humor, she brings joy and laughter to those around her, making her a beloved figure in her family..
