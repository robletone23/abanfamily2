Pablo Rocero Aban

Born: June 29, 1999

Occupation: Building Maintenance Worker

Pablo Rocero Aban, the 9th son of Diosdado and Rosario Aban, was born in Naga, Cebu. He moved to Manila, where he initially pursued criminology, but had to stop due to financial constraints. He then worked as a waiter before transitioning into a career as a building maintenance worker. Pablo is known for his humor, he is a very funny person who continues to work hard to provide for himself and his upcoming baby.
