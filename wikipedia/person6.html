Mario Rocero Aban

Born: January 19, 1986

Occupation: Professional Truck Driver

Mario Rocero Aban, the fourth son of Diosdado and Rosario Aban, was born in Naga, Cebu. After moving to Manila, Mario pursued and completed a degree in Criminology. Despite his academic success, his life took a difficult turn due to struggles with addiction, which deeply affected his journey. Mario's wife is Arlene, with whom he had three sons, two of whom are twins. Though he faced many challenges, Mario has made significant efforts to turn his life around and is now focused on rebuilding his future and making positive changes for himself and his family.
